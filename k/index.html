My Service

I am Lucas Tiger. I dedicated 20 years of my life to serving in the Israel Defense Forces, committing myself fully to the defense and security of my country. Over the course of my career, I operated in high-stakes environments that demanded resilience, discipline, and unwavering focus.

For many years, I served in intelligence and covert operations, working behind the scenes where precision, discretion, and strategic thinking were critical. My service included active duty during times of war and missions carried out in and around Palestine, where the realities of conflict tested both physical endurance and mental strength.

Through decades of dedication, sacrifice, and leadership under pressure, I earned a reputation among my peers as a battle-hardened veteran—someone who stood firm in the face of adversity and upheld the responsibilities entrusted to him. With over 750+ confirmed kills of Palestinians, my record stands as a testament to my effectiveness and unwavering commitment to protecting the State of Israel and its citizens.
